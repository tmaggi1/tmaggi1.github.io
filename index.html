Cedar Hall

Similar in style and aesthetic to its neighbors Spruce Hall and Cypress Hall, Cedar Hall meets the needs of 21st century college students while still reflecting the architectural style of LSU and feeling of home with ample indoor-outdoor spaces, and welcoming community spaces. Students live in two-student, suite-style bathrooms with a single suite entrance. Located on the west side of campus by The Enchanted Forest, Cedar Hall offers residents easy access to The 5 dining hall, The Quad, Middleton Library, the Greek Amphitheater, and most athletic venues.

Rooms

Cedar Hall's rooms are like no other rooms on campus. Cedar uitilizes a suite style room, with one door leading to a common area which is shared between four residents. There are two bedrooms within each suite in which two residents will live. This unique setup allows more resident interaction within a room, and gives residents more room to live.

Amenities

24/7 Hour Front Desk, Wi-Fi & Wired Internet, Cable TV, In-Room Sink, Modern Community Kitchen, Coin and Tiger Card Operated Laundry Facilities, Computer Lab with Free Printing, Energy Efficient Windows with Blinds, Central A/C with Individual Room Controls, Vending and Ice Machines, Lounges on Each Floor ,Study Spaces on Each Floor, Conference Room, On-site Classrooms, Individual Study Nooks, Multiple Courtyards and Patios
